The bottleneck

The grid won't wait. AI data centers can't either.

PJM, MISO, and ERCOT interconnection queues now run 6–15 years. ~2,300 GW sits stuck in U.S. queues — more than the country's entire installed capacity. Behind-the-meter or PPA is the only path that meets an AI timeline. The problem: there's no fast, common yardstick to compare the options. That's this.
