1What this tool is

On-Site Power Intelligence ("the Tool") is a free, browser-based planning estimator for behind-the-meter (BTM) electricity feasibility at AI data center sites. It compares on-site generation scenarios (gas turbine, reciprocating engine, fuel cell, solar + battery, and verified hybrid and nuclear PPA reference cases) using public data from EIA, NREL, SEC EDGAR, GridLab, state PSC filings, and other government and industry sources.

2What this tool is NOT

This tool does not constitute investment, legal, or engineering advice. All outputs are planning-grade estimates intended for early-stage screening only.

  • Not an engineering study. Treat every number as ±40% accurate. Final design requires a licensed engineer.
  • Not investment advice. Capex, lead time, and PPA figures are illustrative ranges, not commitments.
  • Not legal advice. Permitting, zoning, and regulatory pathways must be verified with qualified counsel.
  • Not a vendor quote. OEM pricing and delivery slots change weekly; verify directly with Wärtsilä, Bloom, GE Vernova, Solar Turbines, and others.
  • Not a utility commitment. Grid queue waits are coarse ISO-level estimates; only the serving utility can provide a binding timeline.

Before any commitment, verify every output with the serving utility, OEMs, EPC contractors, and qualified legal counsel.
