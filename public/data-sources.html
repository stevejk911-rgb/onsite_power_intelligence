Structural findings

Five structural findings

  • EIA suppresses fuel-cell capex, Bloom Energy's single-vendor market structure. EIA Construction Cost Data states: "to avoid disclosure of individual company data." → The tool's fuel-cell capex is labeled as a synthetic estimate, ±30%. (FC-003)
  • NREL ATB doesn't track aero gas turbines, its focus is utility-scale. Data-center BTM's sweet spot, aeroderivative (LM2500/LM6000, Solar Titan), is a blind spot. → We trace these via the OEMs directly (GT-001, GT-004/005).
  • NREL ATB and EIA AEO under-state gas CC capex by 26-52%, the GridLab/EFG/Halcyon Sep 2025 report proves this against actual IRP/CPCN filings. Official authoritative data lags the market. (GT-006)
  • NREL caps BESS modeling at 10 hours, explicit in Augustine & Blair 2021 SFS. The tool's previous 12-hour default sat outside the modeled domain → corrected to 6 hours. (SOLAR-003)
  • NREL Cole 2023 → 2025 update revised BESS down 30%, baseline $482/kWh → $334/kWh. NREL itself acknowledged the 2023 estimate was too high. → Introduced the split formula; Solar+BESS corrected by 15%. (SOLAR-004)

Verified deployment cases

200 MW × 2 sites · Williams Companies (Will-Power OH) build & operate for Sidecat (Meta affiliate) · 17 months to completion · 4-OEM mix (Solar Titan 250 × 3 + PGM 130 × 9 + Siemens SGT400 × 3 + Cat 3520 × 15) · physically not connected to grid · OPSB-approved Jun 2025.
DEPLOY-002
Crusoe Abilene Stargate (Texas BTM)
1.2 → 2.1 GW · Lancium's Clean Campus · Stargate $500B JV first site · Oracle/OpenAI + Microsoft added 900 MW · >1 GW gas turbine BTM + grid + wind hybrid · ERCOT verified.
DEPLOY-003
Microsoft + Constellation TMI/CCEC (PA nuclear)
835 MW 20-yr PPA · announced Sep 2024 · Three Mile Island Unit 1 restart (retired 2019) · 2028 online · NRC license extended through 2054 · grid-connected FTM from the start (no BTM attempt) · 3,400 jobs + $3B in state revenue.
DEPLOY-004
Vistra Comanche Peak (TX nuclear)
1,200 MW 20yr + 20-opt PPA · Q4 2027 → 2032 ramp · customer undisclosed ("large investment-grade") · first ERCOT nuclear-data-center PPA · 50% of Comanche Peak's 2.4 GW reallocated · Vistra Adjusted FCF lifted 8-10%.
DEPLOY-005
Talen-AWS Susquehanna (BTM failed)
The decisive BTM-failure case · Mar 2024 $650M Cumulus acquisition (attempted 960 MW BTM) · FERC rejection Nov 2024 (AEP + Exelon opposed, citing $140M/yr cost shift) · Jun 2025 restructured to FTM PPA · $18B, 17 yrs, 1.92 GW grid-connected.

Pattern: All three nuclear deals (DEPLOY-003, 004, 005) are grid-connected FTM. BTM nuclear is effectively foreclosed after the Talen rejection. Gas BTM (DEPLOY-001, 002) is the hyperscaler standard model.
